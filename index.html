Piano Keyboard

Info and tips

This piano keyboard is created with A4 as 440Hz, with computer-generated sound using the JavaScript AudioContext, OscillatorNode, and GainNode.

For easier chromatic scales, use the tab key to progress forward and shift + tab to progress backwards.

For less distractions, use the minimal mode. If you want this as an app on your phone or touch-screen device, save it to your homescreen and it will automatically use the fullscreen mode.

Browser support

This app should work in Firefox, Chrome, and Safari on desktop and on Firefox, Safari, and Opera on iPhone and iPad. Sorry Chrome and Edge mobile, I still haven't figured out why it doesn't work there.
